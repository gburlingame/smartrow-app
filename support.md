# Support

## Contact Us

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email us at [greg@theburl.com](mailto:greg@theburl.com). We typically respond within 24 hours.

You can also [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/gburlingame/smartrow-app/issues).

## Getting Started

1. **Wake your SmartRow** — Give your rower a tug to wake up the sensor
2. **Open SmartRow Companion** — The app will scan for nearby SmartRow devices
3. **Tap to connect** — Select your SmartRow from the device list
4. **Start rowing** — Tap "Start Session" and your metrics will appear in real time

## Pairing a Heart Rate Monitor

1. Go to **Settings** > **Heart Rate Monitor**
2. Tap **Pair Heart Rate Monitor**
3. Make sure your heart rate strap is on and broadcasting
4. Select your device from the list
5. Your heart rate will appear during your next session

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My SmartRow isn't appearing in the device list.**
A: Give your rower a tug to wake up the sensor, then try scanning again. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled in your iPhone's Settings.

**Q: Can I use this app without a SmartRow sensor?**
A: No. SmartRow Companion requires a [SmartRow](https://www.smartrow.fit/) sensor attached to your rowing machine.

**Q: How do I export my workouts to Strava?**
A: Go to Settings > Strava and sign in with your Strava account. Enable "Auto-export to Strava" and your sessions will upload automatically when you end them.

**Q: How do I export to Apple Health?**
A: Auto-export to Apple Health is enabled by default. You can toggle it in Settings.

**Q: Do my sessions sync across devices?**
A: Yes. When signed into iCloud, your rowing sessions automatically sync across all your devices.

**Q: What rowing machines are supported?**
A: Any rowing machine with a [SmartRow](https://www.smartrow.fit/) sensor installed. SmartRow is compatible with most water rowers.
